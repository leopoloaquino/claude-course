Start with judgment, not tool worship

Slide 1 of 4
Core ideaFor this audience, AI fluency means being able to ask better, scope better, and review better. It does not mean pretending everyone became an engineer overnight.

Why this matters

  1. Your team already uses AI, but often inconsistently.
  2. Without structure, outputs become generic or risky.
  3. Fluency reduces rework and improves trust in the process.

Marketing ops example

A campaign ops lead should be able to specify the audience, channels, constraints, required components, and quality checks before asking AI to adapt campaign messaging.
