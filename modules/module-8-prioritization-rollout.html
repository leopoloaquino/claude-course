Not everything interesting deserves attention right now

Slide 1 of 3
Sequencing principleMCP matters. Bedrock matters. Vertex matters. But they matter at the right time, for the right people, in the right stack.

Learn now vs later

  1. Learn now: AI fluency, software basics, app architecture, prompting, Claude Code basics.
  2. Learn later: APIs, skills, small internal tools, beginner-safe agents.
  3. Learn if needed: MCP, Bedrock, Vertex AI.

Strategic payoff

Sequencing prevents fake confidence and keeps the team focused on the highest-return capabilities first.
