Enterprise learning design

A real course, not just a long page of information

This redesign treats the experience like a professional internal academy. The structure is now organized into phases, each module contains multiple instructional layers, and the learner moves through concepts, examples, decision-making, and practical application instead of passively reading. The target learner is an operational marketing team that is becoming more technical, not a room full of engineers pretending to be beginners.

Phase-based designFoundation, workflow literacy, building, and expansion.
Interactive instructionScenarios, builders, quizzes, checklists, and guided practice.
Role relevanceExamples are tied to content ops, localization, campaign QA, reporting, and briefs.
Enterprise toneClear, polished, structured, and designed for team rollout rather than casual self-study only.
